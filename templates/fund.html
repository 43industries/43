Fund

The XRP‑Native Fund

A concentrated, XRPL‑first mandate built for allocators who want real exposure to the XRP ecosystem, without trying to trade every candle.

Mandate

XRP ecosystem + hard assets

We combine a core position in XRP with large‑cap market beta and targeted XRPL infrastructure bets. The objective is to compound with the ledger, not chase every narrative rotation.

  • Core sleeve: XRP conviction, staking / AMM where appropriate.
  • Beta sleeve: BTC / ETH for broader market capture.
  • Infra sleeve: XRPL tokens, L1s and picks‑and‑shovels.

Structure

XRP sleeve design

We size risk around the XRP ledger rather than against it. A typical target mix:

  • 40–50% core XRP.
  • 30–40% BTC / ETH large‑cap beta.
  • 10–20% XRPL infrastructure and other L1s.

Rebalancing is quarterly or on significant moves (>50%), not daily tinkering.
