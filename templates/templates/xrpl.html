XRPL Strategy

The XRP‑Native Playbook

A focused thesis for builders and funds who want to live inside the XRP Ledger: own the narrative, ship useful tools, and put personal capital to work with intention.

1. Fund Narrative

"The XRP‑Native Fund"

Most crypto funds market themselves as BTC / ETH generalists. This reframes your vehicle as a dedicated bet on the XRP ecosystem.

Current positioning Sharper positioning
"Crypto & precious metals" "XRP ecosystem + hard assets"
"Digital assets" "XRPL infrastructure and native tokens"
"Crypto sleeve" "XRP conviction, diversified XRPL exposure"

Why this works: XRP holders are tribal, underserved, and capitalized. They want funds that get it.

2. Personal XRP

Deploy or Hold?

Treat your own stack as strategic fuel, not trading inventory. Align it with your risk tolerance and your build runway.

Option Action Rationale
Hold Cold storage, forget Conviction preserved, minimal friction
Stake / earn AMM pools, lending Yield with smart, sized risk
Deploy into fund Seed your own AUM Skin in the game, confidence for allocators
Fintech runway Pay yourself to build Convert XRP into focused build time

Given this lens — fintech revenue first, then fund growth — the bias is toward holding or light staking. Don’t sell the core stack at shallow levels just to fund experiments.

3. Fintech

Own the XRPL Wedge

You are technical, Cursor‑native, and early. Ship simple tools that make the ledger more legible for operators and capital.

This week Ship
XRPL tool Address explorer, transaction tracker, or AMM calculator
Content "What I learned building on XRPL"
Outreach 5 cold DMs to XRPL grantees

Small, sharp tools plus honest build-in-public content is how you become the default XRPL person in your niche.

4. Portfolio Design

XRP Sleeve Structure

A simple, intuitive allocation that keeps XRP at the core while acknowledging BTC / ETH market beta and higher‑risk XRPL infrastructure bets.

Component Allocation rationale
Core: XRP 40–50% — your conviction, ecosystem exposure
Large‑cap: BTC, ETH 30–40% — market beta, LP comfort
Infrastructure: XRPL tokens, L1s 10–20% — ecosystem plays, higher risk / reward

Rebalancing: Quarterly or on significant moves (>50%), not daily trading.
